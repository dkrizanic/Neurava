# Epic 6 Context: Business-Lite Projects And Future Collaboration Foundation

## Goal

Business-capable users can keep project records and project notes/history separated by Workspace Context, while the model stays ready for future collaboration.

## Stories

- Story 6.1: Business Context Entry For Eligible Users
- Story 6.2: Business Project Records
- Story 6.3: Project Notes
- Story 6.4: Project History Summary
- Story 6.5: Collaboration-Ready Business Model

## Implementation Notes

- Workspace Context switching and company registration were established in Epic 1.
- Projects are workspace-scoped and can be used in Business Context without leaking into Personal Context.
- Notes can link to projects through existing linked resources.
- Project summaries read linked/source notes in the active workspace.
- The model keeps company, membership, workspace, and project boundaries explicit.
